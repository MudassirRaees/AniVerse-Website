One Piece

One Piece The series follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, as he explores the Grand Line in search of the mythical treasure known as the "One Piece" to become the next King of the Pirates.

Dandadan

Dandadan is an anime series that follows two high school students who make a bet to prove the existence of ghosts or aliens. The series combines supernatural elements with personal dynamics, and features comedy and paranormal threats.

Blue Lock Season 2

Blue Lock season 2 is an anime series that follows a group of high school players competing to become Japan's next World Cup striker. The season focuses on Yoichi Isagi and the other players who advanced to the next stage of the Blue Lock program.
